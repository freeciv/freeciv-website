Mailing lists

The Freeciv Project has several email lists open to the general public; everyone is welcome to join.

freeciv-announce
A low-volume mailing list for announcements of general interest, mainly new releases.
freeciv-dev
Freeciv development. Receives also notifications about commits pushed to freeciv repository.
freeciv-i18n
Freeciv localisation (translation).
freeciv-tickets
Patch/bug tracker traffic. Ticket comments are the primary development discussion channel.
